Meet The Team

Oscar Sterling

Hospitality Manager and Head chef

Started working as a chef in London in in heigh end resturants before working for McLaren in Formula 1 and Formula E, travelling the world for around 4 years

I plan big breakfasts on race weekends with a high-car, high-protien for lunch to keep the team fuelled for the day, before mixing it up at night with things they enjoy.

Its important to consider their nutrition throughout the day but also consider people's dietary requirements.

Chris Cheeseman

Head Barman and Food Service

I started with a catering company around 8 years ago before doing a full circuit with Mclaen as head barman. then moved on to Formula E which is where i met Oscar, and extreame sailing and other performance sports

I love meeting people so its great getting to know everyone at an event and throughout the year. I like to be the first person people see and to be able to talk to guests, making sure they are happy and content.

If you can make a small act to make someone feel at home, then they'll enjoy their day even more.

Ivan Tokar

Pastry Chef

I have been a pastry chef for arround 19years - I've always worked at companies where everything is hand made. Every cake and sweet we serve is hand made by us on site. i met Oscar back at McLaren.

When you work at a bigger companies the quality can drop so its ideal to come to a smaller team where we can provide a personalised service is perfect.

Getting feedback from guests every day motivates all of us even more and makes us feel like we are part of the team when we are on site.
