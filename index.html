At MJP Hospitality, we pride ourselves on our passion, precision, flair, creativity and service with style.

Services

Whether the need is for a corporate dinner, wedding catering, christening, drinks reception, canapé evening, champagne and oysters, or a private chef experience, look no further than MJP Hospitality.

Corporate catering

When attention to detail matters, the MJP team can utilise its decades of experience in corporate catering to deliver a high-quality event which will impress guests and clients alike.

Private chef experience

Enjoy the very best of MJP’s expertise with an intimate event to celebrate birthdays, anniversaries, wedding services, dinner parties, or special occasions, making the most of a specially-tailored menu.

Unique events

Having been used to working in a variety of locations without compromising on quality or service, MJP provides event catering to the highest level. No matter your needs and requirements, wherever the event is taking place, the team can deliver.

About


MJP Hospitality is recognised and renowned for its outstanding standards and levels of catering and hospitality services within the motorsport industry, delivering first class experiences at events including the British GT Championship, Porsche Carrera Cup GB, British Touring Car Championship, and Blancpain GT Series.


However, the team at MJP also has knowledge and expertise in a wide variety of event service environments, with the ability to set up its twin-storey awning, and mobile kitchen unit to suit any client’s needs.

Assets


Twin storey motorhome

MJP Hospitality owns a fully mobile articulated trailer which can be used as a luxury hospitality lounge with added office or private space if required. Equipped with internal heating and air-conditioning on both floors, it also has dual television points, double electric entry doors, a premium cocktail bar, bistro seating, twin-terrace viewing platforms, and a professional PA and music system, and can quickly and efficiently be set up by its experienced team.


Premium transport awning

MJP’s premium built awning comes with quality vinyl lines and outers with hard-sided panels for easy graphical display points. Guests will be greeted at our reception unit, comfortable with quality flooring, and enjoy buffet locations and a barista bar.


Specialist mobile kitchen

Equipped with fully stainless-steel preparation areas, MJP’s mobile kitchen has a twin-turbo gas combi convention oven, SIB gas oven, water boilers, and refrigeration. With professional chefs and service, it is suitable for catering a wide range of events in any location.
